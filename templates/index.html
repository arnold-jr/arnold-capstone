AmeniDC

88% of the household of Washington, DC don't own a car, so living near to amenities such as grocery stores, metro stations, and parks is at a premium within the District. The app AmeniDC aims to quantify just what that premium is, allowing users to quickly visualize relationships between proximitity to amenities and the cost of owning a private residence.
